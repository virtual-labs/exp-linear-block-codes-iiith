### Textbooks

**1. Error Control Coding**<br/>
BY: S. Lin and D. Costello - 2nd Edition, Prentice-Hall, 2004<br/>
_Comprehensive coverage of linear block codes, cyclic codes, BCH codes, and decoding algorithms including standard array and syndrome decoding._

**2. Introduction to Finite Fields and Their Applications**<br/>
BY: R. Lidl and H. Niederreiter - Cambridge University Press, 1986<br/>
_Fundamental concepts of finite field theory essential for understanding error-correcting codes and their algebraic structures._

**3. Fundamentals of Error-Correcting Codes**<br/>
BY: W. Huffman and V. Pless - Cambridge University Press, 2003<br/>
_Detailed treatment of linear codes, generator matrices, parity check matrices, and decoding techniques._

**4. A First Course in Coding Theory**<br/>
BY: Raymond Hill - Oxford University Press, 1986<br/>
_Accessible introduction to coding theory with focus on linear block codes and their properties._

**5. The Theory of Error-Correcting Codes**<br/>
BY: F. J. MacWilliams and N. J. A. Sloane - North-Holland, 1977<br/>
_Classic reference on algebraic coding theory covering linear codes, weight distributions, and optimal codes._
